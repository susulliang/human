# V3 — Bearer

**Title:** 《沉默的矿脉：传灯》 / *The Silent Vein: Bearer*  
**Runtime target:** 14–18 min (longer denouement)  
**Logline:** Jark survives the vision and carries Dark’s gospel back to Freetown—where the Murmur still whispers and the war over Cerebrium is just beginning.

**Difference from V1/V2:** Full **bookend** structure; Act III is half the film; romance-adjacent bond stays **intellectual-spiritual** but includes one clear shared scene.

### Scene 5 — Memory with **meeting** (4:45–8:15)

Extended beats:

1. Dark’s loneliness in Branch A (ice palace = Luna Cradle aesthetic).
2. Living Cerebrium discovery.
3. Writing *Cerebrium System* — voiceover reads Article I: *“Symbiosis, not strip-mining.”*

   > **Dark:** You read too fast.  
   > **Jark:** You wrote too slow.  
   > **Dark:** Enceladus is not a mine. It’s a choir. Someone has to listen.  
   > **Jark:** Why me?  
   > **Dark:** Because you hate noise.

6. Death; seeds split—**follow Ceres seed** into Freetown network mesh (visualize symbiotic lights).
7. **Follow abyss seed** to Dream.

### Scene 6 — Dream & instruction (8:15–9:00)

## ACT III — Return to noise (9:00–END)

### Scene 7 — Wreck & survival (9:00–11:00)

- 2.35 returns; jaw bleeding; notebook ruined but **one dry page** in breast pocket (implausible → **miracle beat**, debate on set).
- Deep watchers **visible**—one nods, does not detain.
- He ascends tether **still attached to winch** (auto-recall triggered by breach).

### Scene 8 — Freetown night (11:00–15:00)

- **16mm 1.85 handheld:** same alley as opening; now **months later** or same week—timestamp vague.
- He reads from soaked notebook (copied text from memory) to circle of youths.
- Murmur billboards flicker; his voice **competes**—not wins, **persists**.
- Inserts sticker remnant on wall next to mural; crowd adds second symbol: **listening ear**.

### Scene 9 — Closing (15:00–17:30)

- Jark at shrine; eyes open; **tears** (first emotion shown openly).
- V.O.: *“She sank so we could stay above. I stay above so someone remembers why she sank.”*
- Split screen: left = Freetown firelit chaos; right = vein pulse slow.
- Fade: both sides **equal brightness**—visual thesis “two worlds, one origin.”

**Ending:** Explicit survival; sets up series/episodic potential (Jark vs. cartel scouts).
