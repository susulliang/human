# V2 — Submerge

**Title:** 《沉默的矿脉：沉入》 / *The Silent Vein: Submerge*  
**Runtime target:** 10–12 min (tighter)  
**Logline:** When the hull fails, Jark does not fight the water—he finishes what Dark began and disappears into the vein on purpose.

**Difference from V1:** Same opening through Scene 1; Act III commits to **permanent merger**—no ambiguous wreck smile. Horror-sacrament tone.
