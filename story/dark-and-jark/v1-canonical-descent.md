# V1 — Canonical Descent

**Title:** 《沉默的矿脉》 / *The Silent Vein*  
**Runtime target:** 12–15 min  
**Logline:** A Freetown diver sinks into Enceladus’s living dark to prove Dark was not a fool—and learns the only immortality that matters is the kind you give away.

**Visual thesis:** Fragmented noisy reality (2.35:1 digital / 60fps+) vs. silent truth (IMAX 70mm 1.43:1 3D at contact).

---

### Scene 2 — Forced egress (1:30–3:30)

- Exosuit auto-seals mid-current; tether severed or burning away on winch.
- **Branch A memory flash** (optional 1s): sterile white corridor, 60fps glass-smooth—contrast whip.
- Jark lands on ridge; ahead: first **vein glow**—not LED, **organic pulse**.
- Dark’s whisper enters—not words yet, **reverse echo** under water, like voice heard from inside stone.
- He walks toward light against training (“sample and retreat”).

## ACT II — CONTACT (3:30–8:00)

### Scene 3 — Glove on living ore (3:30–4:00)

- Gauntlet presses crystalline membrane; viscosity like skin.
- **Cut:** frame opens to **1.43:1 IMAX 3D**; color erupts (gold-green biolume).
- Real-world audio **vacuum minute** (~45s subjective; hold 15s screen) then single **whale-heart** pulse.

### Scene 4 — Memory cascade (4:00–7:30)

*Single continuous “myth take” — may be stylized montage inside one moving Steadicam/underwater drone path.*

1. **Branch A lab:** Dark overlooked at council glass; data streams ignore her.
2. **Discovery:** microscope dissolve to **ocean-in-stone**—Cerebrium breathes.
3. **Writing:** fingers carve *Cerebrium System* into light-table; text propagates as network graph.
4. **Severance:** neural ports spark; she tears free; chooses wrinkled hands in Branch B mirror.
5. **Brief meeting:** young Jark in Freetown rain—she passes data-crystal; no romance, **transfer of burden**.
6. **Death & split:** body still; seeds arc—one toward **Ceres lights**, one **down** into same vein.

### Scene 5 — Dark’s Dream (7:30–8:00)

- Colossal crystal silhouette of Dark—**The Silent Vein** made architecture.
- Chorus: Freetown temple hum + Deep cult syllables + her whisper—all **same frequency**.
- Jark floats into spiral light; arms open.

---

## ACT III — RETURN (8:00–END)

### Scene 6 — Rotation & release (8:00–11:00)

- Spiral camera (360° over 90s); embrace = not erotic, **communion**.
- Understanding V.O. (hers or merged): *“You cannot keep yourself and keep the world.”*
- Light contracts; 3D collapses back to **2.35 grain**.

### Scene 7 — Wreck (11:00–13:30)

- Submersible **wreck** on ridge; suit breached but sealed; biolume fading in veins.
- Jark on silt, eyes closed—**small smile** (did he merge a thread of self into stone, or only visit?).
- Hold. Cut to black **before** rescue or absorption answer.
- **Tag audio:** one reverse-echo syllable from crystal, then nothing.
